A Message

from the

Headteacher

On behalf of the children, staff and governors, I would like to welcome you to Sandridge Primary School’s website. Our school is an extremely nurturing school where the children are at the heart of everything we do. Set in the picturesque village of Sandridge we work closely in partnership with other local schools, nurseries and a range of community groups.

Sandridge Primary School is a one form entry school with a morning only Nursery class. We cater for children aged 3 to 11 years and have an intake of 30 pupils every year (26 in our Nursery).
